Who We Are

We are a Canadian vehicle inspection service built to protect buyers from hidden problems, expensive repairs, and risky purchases.

Our mission

At TrustGuard Canada, our mission is to give buyers real clarity before they commit to purchasing a vehicle. We believe people should know exactly what they are paying for.

Our approach

We combine hands-on inspection with diagnostic scanning to uncover the issues that can be missed during a normal walkthrough. We focus on truth, detail, and clear feedback.

What We Do

We inspect used vehicles before purchase so you can avoid hidden issues and make better decisions.

Diagnostic Scan

We scan the vehicle with a special device to check for codes, system faults, hidden warnings, and electronic problems.

Physical Inspection

We inspect everything we reasonably can, including tires, brakes, engine state, visible wear, leaks, and signs of damage or neglect.

Honest Feedback

We tell you what we find in simple language so you understand the condition of the vehicle before spending your money.

How We Do It

Our process is simple, serious, and built to protect the buyer.

Inspection process

  • Review the vehicle visually
  • Run a diagnostic scan
  • Check major mechanical and visible condition points
  • Look for warning signs and costly issues
  • Explain findings clearly and honestly

Why it matters

Many vehicles look good on the surface but hide expensive problems. Our inspections help you negotiate better, avoid risky buys, or walk away before losing money.
